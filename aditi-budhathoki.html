Aditi Budhathoki Wiki, Age, Height, Weight, Boyfriend, Biography &amp; More

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Aditi Budhathoki is a Nepali actress who is much famous for her work in “Kri” which is a regional movie of Nepal. The name of the movie means Revenge. She worked in this movie which comes in the category “romance, drama, and action”. As per the source, the project was written and directed by the same person Surendra Poudel.

The star cast of the movie included Budhathoki, Anmol K.C., Anoop Bikram Shahi, Kameshwor Chaurasiya, and Raju Pal. The budget of the movie was 3.5 crores under which it was made on the box office it earned twice the budget of the movie i.e. 9.8 crores.

The movie gained a lot of appreciation for its cinematography, music, performance, and its original plotline. The movie received the National Film Award of the year 2018 in Nepal for Best Cinematographer.
