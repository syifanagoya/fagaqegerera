Heels Season 2 Release Date Status, Renewed Confirmation &amp; Everything You Need To Know

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

Alexander Ludwig and Stephen Amell used social media to announce that the 2nd season of HEELS, Starz’s new hit wrestling drama, is now being made.

Last year, the first season of the gripping new family drama was a surprise hit for Starz, kicking off the furious competition between Ace (Alexander Ludwig) and Jack Spade (Stephen Amell).

The two leading men, who used to be on Vikings and Arrow, have now posted on Instagram that they’re back on set for the much-anticipated sequel. Heels is an American drama television series. It first aired on Starz on August 15, 2021.

So, when will the next season come out? How does the story go? Who would play the main roles again? Keep reading for more information.

Heels Season 2 Is It Renewed?

Heels Season 2 has been formally renewed for 2022.

Heels Season 2 Release Date

Starz has confirmed that Heels Season 2 Release in 2022. Heels will tap out for Season 2 as Starz has picked up the summertime debut of the rookie wrestling drama.

Trey Tucker and Robby Ramos, who play Bobby Pin and Diego Cottonmouth, respectively, have been promoted from regular to program regular roles for season 2. Next year, production will start.

Heels Season 2 Cast

The major Season 1 cast members are anticipated to return. Alison Luff, Mary McCormack, Chris Bauer, Allen Maldonado, James Harrison Jr., Roxton Garcia, and Kelli Berglund are on the list. Mary McCormack, Kelli Berglund, Allen Maldonado, James Harrison Jr., Roxton Garcia, and Chris Bauer should all make a comeback.

Mike O’Malley will return as the showrunner and executive producer for Season 2 according to Deadline. He will also make a second appearance as Charlie Gully, the promoter of Florida Wrestling Dystopia. Michael Waldron, the series’ original creator, writer, and executive producer, will also return while continuing to collaborate with Marvel Studios and LucasFilm.

Heels Season 2 Plot

According to Starz, the 2nd season of Heels is now being produced in Atlanta, Georgia.

The two co-stars of the popular sports drama have subsequently independently confirmed the news via social media posts from the set.

Heels Crystal won the DWL championship and Ace and Jack had a tense relationship at the end of Season 1. Staci and Jack’s union is currently in trouble as well. The majority of Season 2 will be devoted to Staci and Jack’s attempts to make amends with both his brother and wife.

Since the show really wouldn’t be the same without Ace in the ring, there has also been discussion about whether Ace will end up leaving the DWL, even for a brief time.
