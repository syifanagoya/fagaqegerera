Gigi Gorgeous is a Canadian YouTuber, socialite, actress, producer, and model who rose to fame as she started posting on her YouTube as ‘Gregory Gorgeous’. However, after coming out as a transgender woman in 2013, she began going by ‘Gigi Gorgeous’. Her YouTube channel has accrued a fan base of more than 3 million subscribers. In addition to that, Gigi has also been a part of a variety of projects such as I Hate Myselfie, I Hate Myselfie 2, Project Runway All Stars, The Listener, The Avenue, The Marco Marco Show, The Campus, Bro’Laska, What’s Trending, Not Too Deep with Grace Helbig, Katy Perry Live: Witness World Wide, Shane and Friends, This is Everything: Gigi Gorgeous, Daily Pop, Me and My Grandma, Thread of Man, and Undivided Attention. Gigi has also received many awards including the Critics’ Choice Award, Streamy Award, Shorty Award, and Logo TV Award.
