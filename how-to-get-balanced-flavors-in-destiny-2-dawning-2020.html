How to get Balanced Flavors in Destiny 2 - Dawning 2020

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, April 9, 2024
Recommended Videos

Balanced Flavors is one of the many ingredients you will need to collect for Dawning 2020, Destiny 2’s Christmas seasonal event. You’ll have to make up lots of treats and delivery them to different characters throughout the game.

To get Balanced Flavors, you will need to kill enemies using Sniper Rifles, Scout Rifles, or Bows. We would suggest Scout Rifles over Sniper Rifles, as they have a much better ammo economy, but Bows have the advantage of reliable one-hit-kills on lesser enemies if you fully charge the shot.

You can kill any enemy, in any location, so it is not a bad idea to try and farm some other ingredients while you are getting this one. You can aim for precision kills to try and get some Perfect Taste, and whatever enemy you are killing will also have a chance to drop their own associated ingredient as well.

Keep in mind, none of these are guaranteed drops, it might take a while to get some, especially the weapon-related ones. Find a good Public Event, head to the Altar of Sorrows on the Moon, or hit up the Blind Well for a spot where you will be reliably killing hundreds of enemies in a short space of time.

If you need help finding other ingredients, we have a full guide that will help you out. We also have a guide for every Dawning 2020 recipe, which you should also find useful.
