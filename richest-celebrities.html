What is Slaine's Net Worth?

Slaine is an American hip hop MC and actor who has a net worth of $2 million. Slaine was born in Boston, Massachusetts. He released his debut studio album A World with No Skies in 2011. Slaine has also released the albums The Boston Project in 2013 and The King of Everything Else in 2014.

He has also released the EPs Rich Man, Poor Man in 2006 and Slaine Is Dead in 2016 as well as five mixtapes. Slaine has also collaborated with several other artists including Swollen Members, Everlast, U-God, and Raekwon. As an actor he has starred in the movies Gone Baby Gone, The Town, Killing Them Softly, Girl House, By the Gun, and Central Intelligence.
