Lauren London Net Worth | Celebrity Net Worth

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, May 1, 2024

What is Lauren London's net worth?

Lauren London is an American actress and model who has a net worth of $8 million. Lauren London rose to fame for her performance as Erin "New New" Garnett in the 2006 film "ATL." She has gone on to enjoy a successful film and television career.

Lauren was in a relationship with the rapper Nipsey Hussle at the time of his murder in March 2019. They had one child together. Their child, a son, is one of two children who inherited the bulk of Nipsey's $8 million estate.

Early Life

Born in Los Angeles on December 5, 1984, Lauren London first attended Palisades High School, but ultimately decided to be home-schooled so that she could focus on her acting career.

Alberto E. Rodriguez/Getty Images

Career

She began her professional career appearing in multiple music videos for such artists as, Snoop Dogg, Pharrell, Tyrese, T.I., Ludacris, Common, and Ne-Yo. She then transitioned into film and television work in 2006. London first caught the public's attention with a guest-starring role on "Everybody Hates Chris" in the episode "Everbody Hates Funerals." That same year, London snagged her first film role when she played hip-hop artist T.I.'s romantic interest in the coming-of-age film "ATL." Her performance was met with much critical acclaim and she was nominated for Best Supporting Actress at the Black Movie Awards.

The following year, she landed a role on HBO's "Entourage" playing the character of Turtle's love interest. Later in 2007, she was given a role in "This Christmas" without having to audition, solely based on her impressive performance in "ATL." The same year, she became a spokesmodel for the Sean John woman's collection. In 2008, London landed the role of Christina in season 1 of the reboot of "90210." Her next film role was the character of Ivy in the 2009 film "Next Day Air." She also played the character of Cammy Alcott in the Chris Columbus film "I Love You, Beth Cooper" and appeared on the covers of the magazines King and Jewel.

(Photo by Leon Bennett/Getty Images)

Since then, she has gone back and forth between guest-starring and co-starring roles in such film and television projects as, "Single Ladies", "Reed Between the Lines", and "Madea's Big Happy Family". Lauren was a series regular on the show "The Game". She appeared in a total of 44 episodes between 2013 and 2015. In 203, she co-starred in "Baggage Claim", a film adaptation of the playwright and director David E. Talbert's 2005 novel. In 2016, London starred as the character Ginger in the film "The Perfect Match." 2018 saw London starring in two Lifetime Movies: "Always & 4Ever" and "Poinsettias for Christmas." In 2019, London played the lead character of Vanessa King BET's television show "Games People Play."

Personal Life

London was a bridesmaid at Khloe Kardashian's wedding and has appeared in a number of Kardashian-related television shows.

Lauren has one son with rapper, Lil Wayne. They met when she was 15 years old and were briefly engaged.

Lauren began dating Ermias Asghedom, better known as rapper Nipsey Hussle, in 2013. She gave birth to their first child, a boy named Kross Ermias Asghedom, in August 2016. Tragically, Nipsey was shot and killed on March 31, 2019 outside of his clothing store in Los Angeles.

Real Estate

In January 2020 Lauren paid $1.7 million for a four-bedroom home in Sherman Oaks, California.
