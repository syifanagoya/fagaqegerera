Kyle Fletcher Girlfriend Skye Dolecki: Dating &amp; Relationship

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Love is in the air, more accurately in “the ring,” for Kyle Fletcher, an Australian wrestler. Both Fletcher & his girlfriend, Skye Dolecki, are wrestlers who perform in the All Elite Wrestling.

Many speculated with whom Fletcher might be in a relationship after claims of his split with Dante Martin came forth.

In mid-2023, the two times ‘ATTACK! Tag Team Championship’ went Instagram official with a fellow wrestling diva Skye. 

Fletcher is an Australian professional wrestler currently wrestling for All Elite Wrestling (AEW).

He was born on December 24, 1998, in Sydney, New South Wales. He also made appearances in New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW).

Kyle is a former IWGP Tag Team Champion title and a record-setting two-time champion for Strong Openweight Tag Team for NJPW.

In addition, he has also competed in Revolution Pro Wrestling, where he has held the title of Undisputed British Tag Team Champion twice.

Fletcher debuted in July 2014, wrestling throughout New South Wales for various wrestling companies.

At the Aussie Open, he partnered with fellow Australian wrestler Mark Davis, who belongs to the United Empire stable.

He appeared in All Elite Wrestling and several British Independent promotions, such as Progress Wrestling and Attack! Pro Wrestling.

Fletcher frequently competed in the Australian Independent Circuit, performing for companies like Melbourne City Wrestling.

Kyle Fletcher’s Girlfriend, Skye Dolecki

Skye Dolecki is the girlfriend of two times Undisputed British Tag Team Champion Kyle Fletcher.

Skye Dolecki is an American professional wrestler currently wrestling for All Elite Wrestling (AEW).

Born on October 2, 1999, in Chicago, Illinois, Skye also wrestles for Ring of Honor (ROH).

She is popularly known by her ring name Skye Blue.

In 2017, she debuted in wrestling on the independent circuit in Illinois, having her first match in Premier Pro Wrestling (PPW).

Blue also appeared on National Wrestling Alliance (NWA) and other independent shows.

She made her All-Elite Wrestling (AEW) debut on April 7, 2021, and on September 3, she made her AEW Dark debut. 

She won AAW Women’s Championship and was Ranked No. 74 of the top 150 female singles wrestlers in the PWI Women’s 150 in 2021.

Similarly, On March 2, 2023, she made her Ring of Honor (ROH) debut in the episode of ROH on Honor Club, where she, alongside Madison Rayne, defeated The Renegades.

Blue officially joined AEW in April after wrestling for the company since 2021.

Furthermore, she won various titles, including CSW Women’s Championship, PW Battle Royal Champion, and ZERO1 USA Women’s Championship.

Dating And Relationship Timeline

Kyle Fletcher and Skye Dolecki have recently confirmed their romantic relationship on Instagram.

Although the precise start date is unknown, they are popularly assumed to begin dating in June 2023.

As both Kyle and Skye wrestle for AWE, we can assume that the couple met each other at an Episode of AWE.

Before her association with Fletcher, Skye was in a relationship with Dante Martin, a key player in All Elite Wrestling (AEW).

Skye and Dante teamed up on August 12, 2022, during an episode of AEW’s “Rampage.”

The pair paired up to challenge Tay Melo and Sammy Guevara for the AAA World Mixed Tag Team Titles.

While their relationship is still in its early stages, fans and followers are eager to watch how it develops inside and outside the wrestling ring.
