Who is Elena Kampouris?

Elena Kampouris is an American film and television actress. She is best known for playing Allison Doss in the 2014 film Men, Women & Children. She is also famous for her role as Maya Decker in the American television series American Odyssey (2015).

What are the birth facts of Elena Kampouris?

Elena Kampouris was born on 16 September 1997, in New York City, New York, USA. Her nationality is American and she is of Greek, English, and French ethnicity.

She was raised in Bridgewater, New Jersey by her parents. She is the daughter of Alexander Kampouris and Ivey Kampouris. Her mother is a fashion illustrator and designer, and her father owns a wine store.

Talking about her education history, she attended Gill St. Bernard’s School.

Elena Kampouris: Professional Life and Career

Elena made her acting debut with a small guest role of Constance Girl #2 in one episode of The CW’s series Gossip Girl in 2012. The following year, she appeared as Ivy Murray in the TV film Jinxed (2013). The same year, Kampouris made her film debut in the drama film Labor Day, in which she played Young Rachel McCann.

In December 2013, she signed with the William Morris Endeavor for representation. In 2014, she played Allison Doss in the comedy-drama Men, Women & Children. Later that year, she played Alexia in the comedy drama The Cobbler.

In 2015, Kampouris starred in NBC’s thriller series American Odyssey, in which she played Maya Decker. The following year, she had a supporting role in the romantic comedy film My Big Fat Greek Wedding 2, in which she appeared as Paris Miller. Her Broadway debut was in Les Liaisons Dangereuses in 2016, in which she played the character Cécile Volanges. In 2017, she had a supporting role as Juliet Sykes in the drama film Before I Fall.
