What is Bennett Dorrance's Net Worth?

Bennett Dorrance's net worth is $2.6 billion dollars which makes him one of the richest people in Arizona. Bennet Dorrance graduated with a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Arizona, and inherited his wealth from his grandfather, who founded the very successful Campbell's Soup Company. Much of the company's success was due to elder Dorrance's invention of condensed soup. Bennett Dorrance sits on the board with his sister, who also inherited a portion of the company. Campbell's Soup has expanded over the years and now includes such brands as Pace Salsa, V8 Juice, Swanson broths, and Pepperidge Farm cookies.

The company turns an annual profit of approximately $7.7 billion. Mr. Dorrance also runs DMB Associates. The firm is dedicated to real estate development and is largely focused on planned community developments in Arizona. He also created the Dorrance Scholarship award, which gave $65,000 to twenty-seven Arizona students who were the first members of their families to attend college in 2011.
