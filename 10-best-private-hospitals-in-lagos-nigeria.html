10 Best Private Hospitals In Lagos, Nigeria

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, May 27, 2024

Are you new in Lagos and you wish to know the best private hospitals in Lagos just in case you or a close one need medical attention? Then read this article as you would get to know which private hospitals are the best in Nigeria and the services they offer. No doubt, private businesses in Nigeria do better than their state-owned counterparts in Nigeria – healthcare is not an exception.

So, if you can afford it, you do away with a lot of issues caused by delay and other problem associated with government owned hospitals. The only downside to patronizing the private hospitals in the country is the high cost of getting healthcare from them. Please note, this ranking is not official and not based on any order. You need to do your own research well before reaching any conclusion.

St Nicholas Hospital

St Nicholas Hospital, Lagos is one of the best private hospitals in Lagos, Nigeria. It is located in Lagos Island in Lagos. The St Nicholas Hospital was founded in 1968 by Moses Majekodunmi. This hospital has become a leading organ transplant centre in Nigeria, performing the first renal transplantation treatment, the first kidney transplant in Nigeria and the first paediatric kidney transplant in West Africa.

The hospital provides services such as Medical and Emergency, Intensive Care, General Medicine, Nursing care, Cardiology, Paediatrics, Maternity, Diagnostic Imaging, Clinical Laboratory, Surgery, Radiology, Physiotherapy, Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Oncology, Nutrition and dietetics, Dermatology, Dialysis, Pharmacy and Health Assessment.

Eko Hospital

At Eko Hospital, patients are assured of quality primary, secondary and tertiary health care services. They have first-class equipment in the various fields of Medicine – Paediatrics, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Internal Medicine, Surgery, Dentistry, Ear, Nose and Throat (ENT), Ophthalmology, Electro Encephalography (EEG), Endoscopy, CT-Scanning, Radio-diagnosis, Radiotherapy and Effective Investigative Laboratory.

This hospital prides itself as the only publicly quoted hospital business in Nigeria Stock Exchange. It is no doubt, one of the best hospital in Lagos.

Lagoon Hospital

Lagoon Hospitals, is one of the largest healthcare companies in Nigeria. Established in 1986 under the Hygeia Group, the healthcare company provides a range of primary and tertiary healthcare services.

Lagoon hospitals became the first private hospital in the country to successfully perform an open-heart surgery. The surgery was performed by a team of resident Nigerian healthcare professionals.

Reddington Hospital

The Reddington Healthcare Group is renowned at home and abroad for medical excellence and attracts world-class Specialist Doctors and Surgeons.

Founded in 2006, Redington Healthcare Group has grown over the year with numerous facilities spread across Lagos with its flagship hospital located in Victoria Island. The Reddington Hospital is one of the leading international healthcare provider in Nigeria.

They provide medical services such as renal dialysis, obstetrics and gynecology, pediatrics, surgery, ophthalmology, ENT (Ear, Nose and Throat) surgery, radiology, Gastroenterology and psychiatry.

Gold Cross Hospital

Gold Cross Hospital, is a private multidisciplinary Tertiary Care medical centre established with the aim of providing good quality medical services.

Most of the services rendered at Gold Cross can only be attained highly advanced countries.

Vantage Medical Centre (VMC)

According to the information available on their website, the Vantage Medical Centre’s mission is to deliver qualitative healthcare with a focus on prostate disease, men’s health,and vascular access for dialysis patients.

VMC was established in 1999 by Dr. Ayo Shonibare as a specialty practice to provide comprehensive urological and vascular access services. VMC is committed to providing high quality services to patients.

Blue Cross Hospital

Blue Cross Hospital prides itself as a reputable and client oriented healthcare provider who can provide qualitative and efficient healthcare services with vast years of experience and extremely satisfied individuals, corporate organizations and Health Management Organisations (HMOs).

This hospital was established since 1977 and located in the commercial nerve Centre of Ogba, Ikeja in a purpose built and detached building.

Among others, they provide services such as laboratory services, maternity services, ambulance services, pediatric and dentistry services.

Parklande Hospital

This specialist hospital is located in Surulere. It has a functional and well equipped labour ward and an operating theatre capable of most major surgeries.

Their facilities include: Laparoscopy, diathermy machine, Anaesthetic machine, Hysteroscopy, Colposcopy. If what you are looking for is a good hospital where quality will not be compromised in the country, then, Parklande is a good choice.

Jolad Hospital

Jolad Hospital is one of the top hospitals in Lagos that is popular. Since its establishment for over 3 decades now, this hospital had instilled confident on the people that have patronized them. It is sure one of the best equipped hospital in Lagos.

St Ives Specialist Hospital

One distinguishing service at St. Ives, is the In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF) treatments which are affordable and accessible. St. Ives is a multi-specialist hospital that offers dedicated expert services in all matters of women, children and family health.
