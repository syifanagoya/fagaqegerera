Kevin Turen dies aged 44: Cause of death, obituary, funeral arrangements

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, May 27, 2024

Kevin Turen, a longtime Hollywood producer known for HBO dramas “Euphoria” and “The Idol” has passed away, according to reports. He was born in New York City on August 16, 1979, and died at the age of 44.

In this article, we find out about the details concerning his cause of death, his funeral ceremony, and his obituary.

Kevin Turen Cause of death

Turen, who contributed to the popular HBO series Euphoria and the beloved horror movie X, died over the weekend. However, there is no available information about his cause of death. Among many other projects, he produced the X film series from 2022. He was an American producer of films and television shows.

At Columbia University, where he studied cinema, he earned a degree in English and Critical Film Studies. In 2005, Turen made his producing debut on filmmaker Larry Clark’s indie feature Wassup Rockers.

Kevin Turen’s obituary, funeral arrangements

The funeral and burial arrangements for Kevin Turen will be revealed later because the family will need time to grieve and come to terms with their loss. As soon as the final arrangements for the funeral service are made, they will make sure that everyone is informed.

Kevin Turen was survived by his well-known actress wife Evelina Turen and their two adorable children, Jack and James.
